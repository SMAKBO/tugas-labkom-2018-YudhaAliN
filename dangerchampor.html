Camphor that have considerable benefits in their daily lives also need to be monitored for their use because it is very prone to be a danger to you and your health. Here's examples:
1. Hazards If swallowed or consumed

Camphor is very dangerous if swallowed because he thinks can make a person experiencing convulsions, mental, neurosmuscular hyperactivity and also severe irritation in the body. In addition, patients will also experience irritation of the digestive system that will make nausea, vomiting and diarrhea symptoms are quite severe. children usually become a party that many accidentally swallow camphor because it is used to play. Therefore for those of you who have small children to keep and free boys swallow camphor.


2. Causes of Cancer

According to research that has been done shows children who often inhale air containing a lot of naphthalene will be more at high risk of chromosomal aberration (CA) where the disease has recently become one type of cancer. Actually this naphthalene not only can be found in the cabinet only but also in the exhaust of vehicles and also cigarette smoke. Naphthalene has also been included in the carcinogen class by the International Agency for Research on Cancer. In addition, naphthalene has also been classified into a pollutant better known as policylcyc aromatic hydrocarbons or PAHs.


3. Impact on Children

The impact on children who inhale a lot of camphor or naphthalene is to be able to overcome obesity, low IQ levels and CA diseases. according to research between the use of camphor with CA disease is positively related and is currently increasing. This CA disease should not be trivial because its effect can cause translocation where chromosome turnover or chromosomal exchange of chromosomes in one another will cause genetic disorders and this is important. The presence of abnormalities on the chromosomes will increase people suffering from various types of cancer higher with others.


4. Hazards If Affected Eyes

If the eyes of exposure from camphor quite a lot then it can cause inflammation, irritation and the eyes become redness. In addition, the cornea of ​​the eye can also be affected so that vision will also be a nuisance. (read also: cataract eye characteristics)


5. Skin Hazards

If the powder of camphor skin will cause skin irritation, allergic reactions, burning sensation and irritability. (read also: drug itching).

it can be concluded that camphor has a danger that threatens human health. Therefore, we must use it safely. Here's how to use camphor safely.
1. Avoid as much as possible from the reach of children, especially toddlers who easily swallow objects that look interesting and colorful. In addition, avoid babies from exposure to camphor.
2. In baby wardrobe, no need to use fragrance from camphor. The reason, because chemicals in camphor can trigger the rupture of red blood cells and increase levels of bilirubin in the baby's blood.
3. For you who have sensitive skin, you should place camphor in a container, or wrapped with tissue. This is to minimize direct exposure to your clothes and trigger allergic reactions or skin irritation.
4. In order to avoid you from the risk of health problems, do not take too long in the smell of camphor. Use in moderation and keep it out of reach of pets.
